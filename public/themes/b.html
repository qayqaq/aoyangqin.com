From a potential we can define force as the negative gradient of potential energy. Combined with Newton's second law, the dynamics of a mechanical system follow.

1. Basic Mathematics and Physics

We introduce the Hamiltonian as the total energy of the system, treating position and momentum as independent variables:

H(x, p) = p² / 2m + V(x)

This yields Hamilton's canonical equations, from which conservation of energy follows naturally.
